Research

My research focuses on understanding the emission mechanisms of radio-loud magnetars and their interaction with the surrounding interstellar medium. I analyze radio interferometric observations to investigate compact and transient sources, with particular interest in the connection between magnetars and Fast Radio Bursts (FRBs).
